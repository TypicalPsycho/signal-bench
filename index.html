Interview.OS

Interview.OS is designed to help senior leaders evaluate opportunities more clearly—before, during, and after interviews—by surfacing risk, alignment, and decision signals that are often missed or misinterpreted. The broader platform is under controlled development and evaluation.

Platform beta testing is by invitation only.

A public-use GPT is a limited, early-access interface into Interview.OS. It exposes a small subset of the platform’s approach and is intended for exploration and feedback.
